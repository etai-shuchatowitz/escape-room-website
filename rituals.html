The Rite of Silent Binding

To be performed during the waning crescent. A mirror, three black candles, and silence are required. Speak no words. Let the ink dry on the parchment before sealing.

The Offering of the Hollow Flame

Burn herbs collected under moonless skies. Whisper the names of the forgotten into the ash. Leave nothing behind.
